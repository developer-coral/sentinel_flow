6. Data Security

The Service Provider shall implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect Client information.

At a minimum, the Service Provider shall:

1. Restrict access to authorized personnel.
2. Require multi-factor authentication for administrative accounts.
3. Encrypt sensitive information during transmission.
4. Encrypt production backups.
5. Notify the Client within forty-eight (48) hours of becoming aware of any confirmed or suspected security incident.
6. Cooperate during any investigation.
7. Securely return or destroy Client information within thirty (30) days following termination unless retention is legally required.
